Industry
PROSEZ prepared different base models for 3 types of Businesses according to UN ISIC (International Standard Industrial Classification):

1 Articraft

Artifact Industries manufacture Artifacts, e.g. Consumables, Parts or Equipment.

2 Service

Service Industries work on Services, e.g. Agenting, Telecom or Retail Organizations.

3 Datum

Datum Industries work on data, e.g. Software, Design or Architecture Companies.
These base models are not "Best Practice" but you can change them to your own "Best Harmony by Yourself", very fast. Please take a look on list of Industries in pdf.
